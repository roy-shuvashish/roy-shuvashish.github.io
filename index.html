Short Bio

I'm a Master's student in the Civil Engineering program at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, advised by Dr. Jejal Bathi. My reseach work incorporates numerical simulation of flow hydrodynamics and water quality in various hydrologic scenarios for better watershed management. I am passionate about developing novel tools to support better watershed modeling, data analysis and visualization. My current research interests are Numerical Modeling, Hydrodynamics, Water Quality, and hydrologic modeling. I am a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning (CCVL).

Before starting the master's program at the University of Tennessee, I completed my undergraduate education at Bangladesh Univerity of Engineering and Technology (BUET) where I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering degree in September 2015.
